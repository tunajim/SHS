Welcome to SHS

Thank you for visiting the Students Helping Students website. We are a group of Student IT workers providing additional tech support for our student peers here at Washington State University, Vancouver.

Our Motivation - As students, we know the importance of functional technology to get class work done. We believe that as a WSU Vancouver student, you have the right to ask for help with any of your devices and as trained technicians, we’ll try our best to fix them.
